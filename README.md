You were once among the greatest wizards alive - until a forbidden spell tore the Words of magic from your mind. Now you awaken in an unfamiliar world with only fragments of your former power. The missing Words are scattered across the land, buried among precious resources and guarded by dangerous creatures.

Explore, gather, craft, and fight your way through a procedurally generated 2D fantasy world. Every recovered Word expands the spells you can create and brings you one step closer to discovering who erased your past.

## What awaits you

- **Build spells from Words** - combine a modifier, an element, and a form to shape each attack. Pierce enemies with a Frost Shard, unleash a Splitting Ember Wave, fire a Stunning Lightning Beam, and discover many more combinations.
- **Explore a different world each time** - travel through grasslands, frozen tundra, scorching desert, and the mysterious Amethyst Rift. Choose a custom world seed or let the game surprise you.
- **Gather and craft** - chop trees, mine ores, forage for food, and turn your resources into tools, weapons, armor, potions, meals, and useful placeable items.
- **Grow stronger** - defeat biome-specific enemies, improve your equipment, gain levels, and recover Memory to unlock new spell Words.
- **Survive the journey** - manage health, mana, and hunger as the day turns to night. Place a bedroll to establish a safe return point before venturing farther.
- **Recover what was lost** - defeat is not the end. Return to your marked death chest, reclaim your belongings, and prepare for another expedition.

## The spellcrafting system

Magic is spoken in three-part phrases:

1. **Modifier** - changes how the spell behaves, such as *Piercing*, *Stunning*, *Exploding*, *Reclaiming*, or *Splitting*.
2. **Element** - determines its magical nature: *Lightning*, *Poison*, *Frost*, *Ember*, or *Dark*.
3. **Form** - gives the spell its shape: *Shard*, *Beam*, *Wave*, or *Barrage*.

Equip a weapon, then press the displayed number key for each part of the phrase. Your three selections are cast immediately, so experiment with different combinations and adapt your magic to the enemies and biomes ahead.

## Controls

| Action | Control |
| --- | --- |
| Move | `WASD` or arrow keys |
| Mine / gather | Hold left mouse button |
| Interact / use / place item | Right mouse button |
| Select hotbar slot | Mouse wheel or click a slot |
| Build and cast a spell | Number keys `1`–`5` for each Word stage |
| Open inventory and equipment | `E` |
| Open crafting | `C` |
| Open map | `M` |
| Drop one selected item | `Q` |
| Drop the entire selected stack | `Left Ctrl` + `Q` |
| Close a panel / pause | `Esc` |
| Advance the introduction | `Space` |
